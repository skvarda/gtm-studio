# GTM & Narrative Design Doc — Round 19

## 1. The Viral Hook: Victory Screenshots

Every victory path ends with a **stat card** designed for screenshotting. Four distinct endings means four distinct flex posts. The format:

```
┌─────────────────────────┐
│  [VICTORY TITLE]        │
│  ························│
│  Day 47 · $52,400 cash  │
│  12 employees · 63 custs│
│  Burn: +$1,200/day      │
│  ························│
│  "You built something." │
│  ── SaaS Startup Sim ── │
└─────────────────────────┘
```

Each card uses the path's tint color as background. IPO Glory = gold. Profit Machine = green. R&D Utopia = cyan. World Domination = red. The stat lines reveal one by one with a typewriter tick — the pause before each number builds tension. Players will screenshot the moment the final stat lands. That's the share moment.

## 2. Key Narrative Beats

- **Title screen with Continue**: `"Continue (Day X) — your startup didn't die overnight"` — dry, honest, funny.
- **Victory trigger**: The co-founder stands up from his desk for the first time. That single animation IS the story beat. No fanfare until he moves.
- **Victory text per path**:
  - IPO Glory: `"The bell rings. You don't feel different."`
  - Profit Machine: `"Ramen profitable. Then real profitable. Then boring profitable."`
  - R&D Utopia: `"You shipped the thing you actually wanted to build."`
  - World Domination: `"They wrote a blog post about you. It was mostly wrong."`

## 3. Co-Founder Evolution

Subtle only. He never speaks during normal gameplay. But after Day 15, a second monitor appears on his desk with a purple flicker. Interact with it once:

> `"He's watching a live dashboard. Metrics you've never seen. He closes the tab."`

That's it. 27 words. One flag. The implication is the payload — he knows more than you do. No resolution this round.

## 4. Tone-Capturing Dialog

When a player wins via Profit Machine:

> `"Your co-founder nods once. That's the most emotion he's shown in 30 days."`

## 5. Startup Tropes to Incorporate

- **Burn clock as tension**: The day counter in the stat card is the real score. Lower day count = bigger flex.
- **The "what does my co-founder do" meme**: Universal founder anxiety, played completely straight as game mystery.
- **Screenshot-as-receipt culture**: Stat cards mimic the VC tweet format — numbers, one-liner, logo. Players will post these unironically.
