**Round 24 Narrative & Dialog — Key decisions:**

- **Tone anchor**: Recognition humor. Dry, specific, no inspiration-poster energy. Players laugh because they've lived it.
- **Beat 1** — First menu open fires a one-liner: *"Data is not strategy. But it's a start."* One-time only.
- **Beat 2** — Red border below 15 days runway. No words. Color does the work.
- **Beat 3** — At 8 days or fewer: *"Your Series A deck said 18 months of runway."* One line, then silence.
- **Beat 4** — Co-founder sprite rotates one frame toward player when MRR = 0. No dialog. The restraint is the point.

**UI copy philosophy**: The confirm dialog says *"Shut it down? This can't be undone."* — startup shutdown language baked into the menu layer, not the narrative layer. The screenshot moment (4-day runway, red panel, two-person team) is designed to be the shareable artifact without any extra annotation needed.
