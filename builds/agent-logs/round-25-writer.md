# Round 25 — Narrative & Dialog Doc

---

## Story Beats

**The Interruption Economy.** The office has a new ping pong table. Nobody agreed on it. It just appeared. The co-founder was nearby when it arrived. Coincidence.

**Dev Arc:** Every interaction costs focus. The player discovers this the hard way — a casual "how's it going?" tanks afternoon output. The dev isn't rude. Just... gone somewhere inside.

**Sales Arc:** Every interaction is fuel. Touch base three times and watch the salesperson become a force of nature. They needed someone to believe in them today. You're that person now.

---

**Developer — Interaction 3 (hard debuff):**

**Salesperson — Interaction 1:**
> *"Pipeline's looking thin but I've got three calls today. Watch."*

**Salesperson — Interaction 2 (buff triggers):**
> *"I'M ON FIRE. Did you see that close? Did you SEE that?"*

**Salesperson — Interaction 3 (hard buff):**
> *"Call me again tomorrow. I need to hear you believe in me again."*

**Co-founder — Interaction 3:** *(chair rotates 15 degrees. No dialog. The mystery deepens.)*

- Productivity buff notification: *"Sales is activated. (+20% output)"*

---

## Tone Guidelines

Play it straight. The absurdity lives in the mechanics, not the jokes. Let the dev's quiet devastation and the salesperson's manic energy speak without winking at the camera. Founders will recognize it. That's the hook.
