# Creative Brief: Round 25 - Angel Investor Contact

**Priority:** `angel_investor_gina`  
**Target Emotional Beat:** Growing pressure and external validation tension

## Tone Direction

**Primary:** Cautious optimism with undertones of "too good to be true"  
**Secondary:** Building momentum vs. maintaining control tension  

The angel investor outreach should feel like a legitimate opportunity that comes with strings attached. This isn't pure good news—it's a decision point that could accelerate or complicate the player's journey. Jordan's reactions should reflect their mysterious background knowledge about the investor ecosystem.

## NPCs Speaking This Round

**Jordan** (apartment location)
- React to angel investor email with insider knowledge
- Drop cryptic hints about investor ecosystem dynamics
- Show both excitement and wariness about external money
- Continue mysterious background thread (why do they know so much about VCs?)

## Content Requirements (MUST Include)

### Angel Investor Email
- Professional but warm outreach from established angel
- Mentions specific interest in player's market/approach
- Includes subtle urgency ("limited time in market")
- Offers 3 role-variant meeting options:
  - **Seller track:** Focus on go-to-market strategy and customer acquisition
  - **Dev track:** Technical due diligence and product architecture  
  - **Marketer track:** Brand positioning and market opportunity

### Jordan Dialogue
- Immediate reaction to angel email (event trigger)
- Knowledge about this specific investor that seems too detailed
- Warning about "strings attached" to angel money
- At least 2 interaction opportunities to probe Jordan's investor knowledge

### Email Ecosystem
- 1-2 additional emails (spam/newsletters) for inbox realism
- Mom email checking in about "business meeting" if angel responds
