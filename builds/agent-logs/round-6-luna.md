# Creative Brief: Day 1 Content
**Round Priority:** day1_content  
**For:** Sage (Writer) & Felix (Game Designer)

## Tone Direction
**Primary:** Warm and optimistic with undercurrents of nervous energy. Think "first day of school" excitement mixed with "oh god what have we done" realization.

**Voice:** Conversational, slightly caffeinated, relatable. Jordan speaks in startup shorthand but with genuine care. Email copy ranges from cheerful-professional to overeager-bro depending on sender.

**Emotional gradient:** Start cozy → build anticipation → end with "the real work begins now" energy.

## NPCs Speaking This Round
- **Jordan** (primary voice) - Co-founder in apartment, all role variants needed
- **Email senders** (David Chen, Rebecca Martinez, Chad introduction)

## Emotional Beat Target
**"The Leap"** - Player should feel they've crossed from dreaming about a startup to actually running one. The moment when Jordan asks about their role should feel like stepping off a cliff into something amazing and terrifying.

**Success metric:** Player thinks "okay, I'm really doing this" and immediately wants to see what Day 2 brings.

## Must Include
1. **Role selection flow** - Jordan's question with 3 distinct variants (seller/dev/marketer) that feel meaningfully different
2. **Apartment interactions** - Laptop and whiteboard moments that build world/character
3. **Chad introduction** - Email that establishes him as future rival without being heavy-handed
4. **Startup Gazette** - Format the day 1 headlines into an actual readable news digest
5. **Runway anxiety** - Jordan's 18-month countdown should feel real, not abstract

## Explicitly Out of Scope
- ❌ Any NPCs beyond Jordan (Chad appears via email only)
- ❌ Locations beyond apartment 
- ❌ Game mechanics tutorials (save for engine rounds)
- ❌ Heavy exposition dumps about the business plan
- ❌ Multiple conversation trees per object (keep interactions focused)
- ❌ Foreshadowing Jordan's secret (too early, establish baseline relationship first)

## Key Constraints
- **Location limit:** Apartment only (laptop, whiteboard, Jordan interactions)
- **NPC limit:** Jordan present, others via email/text only
- **Day structure:** This is character creation + first morning, not a full day cycle

## Success Criteria
Player should finish this round with:
1. Clear role identity selected
2. Basic understanding of Jordan as co-founder
3. Sense of the startup world they're entering
4. Eagerness to start "the real work" tomorrow

**Tone check:** If it doesn't feel like the opening of a cozy-but-ambitious indie game, revise.
