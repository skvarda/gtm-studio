Written to `docs/mechanics-round17.md`. Key design decisions:

- **Four victory paths** with concrete thresholds, evaluated in priority order (IPO hardest, Profit rewards restraint, R&D rewards focus, Domination rewards grind)
- **Burn rate as difficulty lever**: $500/day base + $300/employee creates a hire-or-die tension where every employee accelerates both victory and death
- **Three progression gates** at Days 1-7, 8-14, and 15+ controlling hire slots, event frequency, and the purple monitor unlock
- **"The Stand-Up"** as the engineered moment: one frame swap after 15-60 days of the co-founder doing nothing. Maximum payoff from minimum animation budget
- **Victory dialog**: four lines, each under 10 words, each arguing a different definition of success

The thresholds are tuned so a skilled player can IPO around Day 30-35, Profit Machine around Day 25, R&D around Day 28, and Domination is the long grind at Day 45+. No-hire runs are viable but lock you into Profit Machine only.
