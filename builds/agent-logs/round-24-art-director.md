**Round 24 Art Direction — Key Specs**

**Panel**: 256x192px centered on the 320x224 canvas. Double-line border (`#4caf50` outer / `#0a0a18` inner). Deep navy fill `#1a1a2e`. No gradients.

**Text**: Existing pixelFont at 8px cap height. Off-white `#e8e8c8` for body, bright green `#52d45a` for headers. All-caps labels, mixed-case values.

**Runway color logic**: green `#52d45a` > 30 days, yellow `#f0c040` 15–30 days, red `#e84040` < 15 days — and that red value pulses at 1Hz when critical. Everything else is static.

**Employee chips**: small `[NAME]` tags, role-colored — dev blue `#5ba3e8`, sales orange `#e87d3e`, 60% opacity fill. Four per row.

**Animation budget is tight on purpose**: panel slides in over 8 frames, cursor blinks at 500ms, runway pulses when danger. Nothing else moves. This is a pause screen — the world should feel frozen.

**Easter egg**: Cash exactly `$0` relabels the row to `VIBES` and shows `$0 (RAMEN BUDGET)` in yellow. One conditional, maximum startup absurdity.
