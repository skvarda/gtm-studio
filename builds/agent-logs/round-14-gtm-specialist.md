# GTM & NARRATIVE DESIGN DOC — Round 14

## 1. THE VIRAL HOOK

**"My co-founder hasn't done anything in 47 days."** The game's viral moment is the screenshot/clip of the co-founder NPC sitting at his terminal doing absolutely nothing while your cash burns. Every founder has had this anxiety. Twitter/Reddit will do the rest. The title screen's retro aesthetic is the second hook — people share pixel art. The combination of "relatable startup pain" + "SNES nostalgia" is the content engine.

## 2. KEY NARRATIVE BEATS THIS ROUND

- **Beat 1 — The Angel Check**: Intro dialog. An investor slides a check across a diner table. "Don't spend it all on ping pong tables." Sets the stakes, teaches the player money matters.
- **Beat 2 — Day 1 in the Office**: You and the co-founder stand in an empty office. He says one line, then walks to his terminal and never explains himself again.
- **Beat 3 — Product Launch (v1.0)**: Hitting 100% progress triggers a celebration. Revenue doubles. The co-founder turns around in his chair for the first time. Says nothing. Turns back.

## 3. CO-FOUNDER EVOLUTION

Keep him ambient and unsettling this round. He should now face his terminal correctly (bug fix aligns with narrative). At product launch, he briefly faces the player — one frame, no dialog. Plant the seed: his terminal occasionally flickers when the player isn't adjacent. No explanation yet. Late-game payoff lives in Rounds 20+.

## 4. SIGNATURE DIALOG

**[Angel Investor, Intro Sequence]:**
*"Here's $500K. That's about 18 months of runway, or 3 months if you hire anyone from San Francisco."*

## 5. STARTUP TROPES TO INCORPORATE

- **Burn rate anxiety** as the core resource tension (already implemented via cash drain)
- **"Ship it" culture** — the v1.0 milestone should feel like relief, not perfection. Dialog: "It's full of bugs. Ship it."
- **First customer dopamine** — post-launch, the first MRR tick should get a special one-time notification: "Someone is paying you money. On purpose."
- **The pivot whisper** — at low cash + low product progress, a random event hints you could pivot. Seed for future rounds.
